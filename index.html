What are cells ?

* Cells are the basic,structural and Fundamental unit of all living organisms.

- All living organisms included animals,birds,insects and human are made up of cells and even a plant is also made up of a cells.

Are Animal cells and Plant cells are same in structure and in function?

* The answer to this question is 'No'.

- Here is the difference between plant cell and animal cell.

Plant cell

  1. In addition to cell membrane,Plant cells also have a cell wall which is the most outer covering layer of the cell which is rigid.
  2. There is large vacuole present in it. About 80 to 90 % place is occupied by it.
  3. Due to the vacuole present in it,the nucleus is pushed to one side in cell.
  4. Plastid-chloroplasts containing chlorophyll is present in it.
  5. They do not chage the shape as they are rigid.
  6. Lysosomes are absent in plant cells.
  7. Plant cells do not burst while kept in hypotonic solution due to the rigidity of cell wall.

Animal cell

  1. There is only cell membrane present in it.
  2. In this,Vacuoles are generally small.
  3. There is lack of chloroplasts in it.
  4. They can change there shape.
  5. Lysosomes are present in it.
  6. Animal cells may burst while kept in hypotonic solution.
